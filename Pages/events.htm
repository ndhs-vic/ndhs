BOOK AUCTION

DECEASED ESTATE PRIVATE LIBRARY AUCTION

        On behalf of the Newstead & District Historical Society

 SUNDAY, 4 MARCH 2007AT 11AM

To be held at the Newstead Community Centre, Newstead

Comp: 3,500 volumes into 500 lots 19th & 20th C. books on Botanical, Birds, Dogs, Wildlife, British country & townships, British & European Monarchs & Social History, Australiana, French Revolution, 1st & 2nd World Wars, The Arctic & Antarctic Regions, Craftwork, Scotland, Cricket and Literature incl: first editions, private press & limited edition books, the majority of which are illustrated with colour or black & white plates.
