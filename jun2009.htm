NEWSLETTER JUNE 2009

  

As Dawn our secretary has taken a well earned overseas holiday, this is a short update of our activities during June.

 

Gordon Dawes

We were saddened to learn of Gordon's death. He was one of our representatives on the CHAA. He will be greatly missed. Janet Trudgeon conveyed our condolences to his family.

 

Thomas Martin Letter

The manuscript is now being finalised for publishing by Graffiti Publications.

 

Future Excursions

At our last meeting we discussed:-

A tour of the Harcourt Museum led by George Milford;

Asking Garry Powell to lead an excursion connected with the Powell family.

 

NEXT MEETING

Monday 20 July at 1.30 pm at the Court house.
